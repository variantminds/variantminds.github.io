Employing Variant Minds

Silvino Rodrigues

Employing Variant Minds offers a comprehensive guide to tapping the unique strengths of Autistic, ADHD, and AuDHD professionals. It explains the conditions, and then challenges outdated HR methods, sharing practical steps to reshape hiring and support processes. Real-world examples and actionable tools show how inclusive workplaces spark greater creativity, productivity, and success for everyone.

Loving Variant Minds

Silvino Rodrigues

Loving Variant Minds is an insightful guide for couples who want to understand and nurture the distinctive dynamics of romantic relationships involving Autism, ADHD, or AuDHD. Whether you are dating, engaged, or married, the book provides practical tools, real‑life stories, and emotionally intelligent guidance to help you celebrate the highs and navigate the challenges of loving a neurodivergent partner.
