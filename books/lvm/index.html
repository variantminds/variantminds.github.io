About the Book

Loving Variant Minds explores the unique emotional landscapes of relationships involving Autism, ADHD, and AuDHD. Written for partners, parents, siblings, and friends, this book blends practical advice with real-world insight to help readers understand and connect with the neurodivergent people in their lives. It breaks down common misunderstandings, highlights the strengths of variant minds, and offers tools for building more respectful, resilient, and fulfilling relationships.
